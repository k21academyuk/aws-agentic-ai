Loan Eligibility FAQ:
Q: What is the maximum loan I can get?
A: Generally up to 60 times your monthly income, subject to credit profile.

Q: Does a low credit score mean rejection?
A: Scores below 650 are typically rejected for personal loans. 650-750 get standard rates. 750+ get the best rates.

Q: How long does approval take?
A: Eligibility is instant. Final approval takes 2-3 business days after document verification.

Q: Can I prepay my loan?
A: Yes. Salaried borrowers pay no prepayment charges after 12 EMIs. Self-employed pay 4% on outstanding.
